Coimbatore, India
Coimbatore, a vibrant city in Tamil Nadu, is a hidden gem in Southern India. Nestled amidst the Western Ghats, it offers a unique blend of culture, nature, and industry. Known as the "Manchester of South India," Coimbatore boasts a thriving textile industry and is a shopping paradise. Its diverse cultural heritage is reflected in the coexistence of various religious sites.
